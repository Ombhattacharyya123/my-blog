sri sri academy

Nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, amidst lush green tea gardens. Sri Sri Academy provides a serene and stress-free environment for children to engage in learning and developmental activities. Sri Sri Academy, is an English-medium, Co-educational School, geared to nurture and educate students from Pre-Primary to Senior Secondary. We provide both day boarding and residential facilities. Our state of art infrastructure, activities and exposure enables every child’s physical, cognitive, emotional, social and spiritual development.
